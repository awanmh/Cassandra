EXCLUDED VULNERABILITIES (Do Not Report):
- API key disclosure without proven business impact
- Wordpress usernames disclosure
- Pre-Auth Account takeover/OAuth squatting
- Self-XSS that cannot be used to exploit other users
- CORS misconfiguration on non-sensitive endpoints
- Missing cookie flags / security headers
- CSRF with no or low impact
- Presence of autocomplete attribute on web forms
- Reverse tabnabbing
- Rate-limit bypassing
- Password complexity/expiration policies
- Clickjacking without proven impact
- CSV Injection
- Sessions not being invalidated (logout, 2FA)
- Email spoofing (SPF/DMARC/DKIM)
- Username/email enumeration
- DoS/DDoS attacks
- Vulnerabilities on out-of-date software (unless <14 days from patch)
- Blind SSRF without proven business impact (pingbacks are not sufficient)
